# Reminder Schedules

## General Reminders
- **Standard:** Reminders sent 1 day before deadlines/approvals.
- **Escalation:** Overdue items escalated to CEO after 24 hours past deadline.

## Task-Specific Reminders
- Reminders for tasks with due dates will be generated dynamically.

## Current Status
- No specific recurring reminder schedules are currently set beyond the standard policy.
